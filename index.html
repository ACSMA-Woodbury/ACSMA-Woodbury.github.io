Woodbury University ACS-Media Arts

Spring Showcase 2021

Please join us on Friday April 30th from 5-6pm for a celebration of our graduating thesis students as well as selected work from ACS-Media Arts spring studios. Student videos, games assets, objects and experiences will be displayed in virtual environment modeled after the former WUHO gallery of the School of Architecture. It will be a fun an interactive event that can experienced on a head set, on a computer, or through a mobile device.
